## Props

| Parameter description type default value|
| :---: | :------: | :-------: | :---------: |
| placement | where the tooltip appears | `top | right | bottom |left` | `top`
| color | tooltip background color | `same as bgcolor of css` | `rgba(0,0,0,.8)`
